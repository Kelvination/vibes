## üí° Key Insights from Research

1. **Niche > Generic**
   - Specific bot for 1,000 servers beats generic bot for 100,000 servers
   - Market depth > market breadth

2. **Churn is Real**
   - Average monthly churn: 3-5% for paid tier
   - Retention requires continuous feature updates

3. **Email = Moat**
   - Collect emails from day 1
   - Email list is most valuable asset
   - Email ‚Üí future customers, partnerships, data

4. **Free First, Paid Later**
   - Ship free to get critical mass (100+ servers)
   - Then add paid tier when demand proven
   - Conversion rates improve over time

5. **Distribution > Product**
   - Perfect bot with no distribution: $0
   - OK bot with great distribution: $5K/month
   - Product matters, but distribution matters more

6. **Discord First, Slack Later**
   - Discord: Fast feedback, quick iteration
   - Slack: Slower but higher ARPU
   - Launch Discord, validate, then Slack

7. **Speed Compounds**
   - Launch in Week 1 > perfect launch in Week 8
   - Feedback from 10 users > perfection in vacuum
   - Iterate based on real feedback
