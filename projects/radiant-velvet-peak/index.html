How to Play

Drive: W/↑ throttle · S/↓ brake (reverse at low speed) · A,D/←,→ steer · Enter respawn at checkpoint · Backspace instant restart · C camera · G ghost · F2 tuning panel. On mobile: on-screen steer, gas and brake buttons.

Handling: at speed the car understeers — brake while turning and the rear rotates you into the corner. Catch it with counter-steer; release the brake to recover.

Wall energy (ERS): glowing cyan strips on walls are hug zones. Each pays once per run, scored by your closest approach at speed — millimeters pay the most. Touching the wall voids the zone. The ERS bar is a passive boost: whatever you've banked continuously raises your power and top speed, and slowly bleeds away — so keep hugging walls to stay fast. No button to press.

The racing line: corners reward outside–inside–outside. Enter wide hugging the outer wall (entry zone on the segment before the turn), clip the glowing apex zone on the inside wall — the main payout — then run wide again across the exit zone on the segment after the turn.

Editor: click to place · R rotate · right-click delete · 1/2/3 toggle Entry/Apex/Exit zones on a hovered curve · Ctrl+Z/Y undo/redo · T test drive · finish a test run to set the author time (medals derive from it) · middle-drag/Shift-drag pan · wheel zoom · Q/E rotate camera. The green guide line at each corner shows the ideal racing line the zones reward; entry/exit zones are placed automatically on the segments before and after each turn.
