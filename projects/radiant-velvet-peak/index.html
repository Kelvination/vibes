How to Play

Drive: W/↑ throttle · S/↓ brake (reverse at low speed) · A,D/←,→ steer · Space handbrake · Shift deploy ERS (hold) · E deploy ERS (toggle) · Enter respawn at checkpoint · Backspace instant restart · C camera · G ghost. On mobile: on-screen steer, gas, brake and ⚡ deploy buttons.

Handling: at speed the car understeers — brake while turning and the rear rotates you into the corner. Catch it with counter-steer; release the brake to recover. Tap Space (handbrake) to snap the rear out and clip a tight apex.

Wall energy (ERS): glowing strips on walls are hug zones (the hotter green ones are higher-paying apex zones). Each pays once per run, scored by your closest approach at speed — millimeters pay the most. Touching the wall voids the zone. Hugging banks energy in the ERS bar; deploy it with Shift (hold) or E (toggle) for a sustained engine boost — spend it on a straight to bank a faster lap.

The racing line: corners reward outside–inside–outside. Enter wide hugging the outer wall (entry zone on the segment before the turn), clip the glowing apex zone on the inside wall — the main payout — then run wide again across the exit zone on the segment after the turn.

Editor: click to place · R rotate · right-click delete · 1/2/3 toggle Entry/Apex/Exit zones on a hovered curve · Ctrl+Z/Y undo/redo · T test drive · finish a test run to set the author time (medals derive from it) · middle-drag/Shift-drag pan · wheel zoom · Q/E rotate camera. The green guide line at each corner shows the ideal racing line the zones reward; entry/exit zones are placed automatically on the segments before and after each turn.

Editor on mobile: drag to pan, pinch to zoom. Line the cyan crosshair up over a cell, then tap Place or Erase. ⟳ Rot rotates the piece, Z1/Z2/Z3 toggle a placed block's zones, ↶/↷ undo/redo. Curves come in three radii — mix tight corners and long sweepers for flow.
