How to Play

Drive: W/↑ throttle · S/↓ brake (reverse at low speed) · A,D/←,→ steer · Enter respawn at checkpoint · Backspace instant restart · C camera · G ghost · F2 tuning panel

Handling: at speed the car understeers — brake while turning and the rear rotates you into the corner. Catch it with counter-steer; release the brake to recover.

Wall energy (ERS): glowing cyan strips on walls are hug zones. Each pays once per run, scored by your closest approach at speed — millimeters pay the most. Touching the wall voids the zone. Deploy stored energy with Shift (hold) or E (toggle) for sustained extra power.

Editor: click to place · R rotate · right-click delete · 1/2/3 toggle hug zones on a hovered curve · Ctrl+Z/Y undo/redo · T test drive · finish a test run to set the author time (medals derive from it) · middle-drag/Shift-drag pan · wheel zoom · Q/E rotate camera.
