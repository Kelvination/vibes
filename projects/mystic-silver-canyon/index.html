How to Play

  • Goal: Get 4 pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) that share at least one attribute
  • Attributes: Tall/Short, Light/Dark, Square/Circle, Hollow/Solid
  • Twist: Your opponent chooses which piece you must place!
  • Turn 1: Player 1 selects a piece for Player 2
  • Turn 2+: Place your piece on the board, then select a piece for your opponent
