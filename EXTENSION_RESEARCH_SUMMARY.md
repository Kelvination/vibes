### Must Do:
1. **Solve ONE specific problem really well** - Not everything
2. **Validate with real users first** - Before building
3. **Freemium with clear value prop** - 20-30% gated, not 80%

### Must Avoid:
- ‚ùå Building for feature creep
- ‚ùå Aggressively monetizing from day 1
- ‚ùå Ignoring user feedback
- ‚ùå Tracking without consent
- ‚ùå Letting the extension go stale
- ‚ùå Misleading store listings

## üéØ Decision Framework

**Ask yourself:**

1. **Is this a real problem?**
   - Could you validate this with 10 users saying "yes"?
   - Do existing solutions fail at something specific?

2. **Can you build an MVP in 2-4 weeks?**
   - Does the feature scope fit?
   - Is technical execution clear?

3. **Will users pay (or see affiliate offers)?**
   - Is the user demographic willing to spend?
   - Are there relevant affiliate partners?

4. **Can you commit 3+ months?**
   - After launch, can you iterate based on feedback?
   - Can you respond to reviews/issues daily for first month?

5. **Is this better than existing solutions?**
   - What's your unique angle?
   - Why would users choose you over competitors?

**If you answered YES to all 5:** Go build it!
**If you answered NO to any:** Revisit your idea first.
